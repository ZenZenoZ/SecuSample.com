Who We Are:

   We are a group of dedicated students who are passionate about making cybersecurity education accessible to all. Our team is composed of individuals from various backgrounds and expertise, but we all share a common goal: to inspire and train the next generation of cybersecurity leaders.
   Our members come from different universities and colleges, and we are united by our commitment to promoting cybersecurity awareness and education. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn about cybersecurity, regardless of their background or resources. That's why we are dedicated to creating programs and resources that are affordable, engaging, and easy to understand.



About the Campaign:

   Our campaign aims to raise cybersecurity awareness in Pateros City, a vibrant and growing community in the Philippines. We believe that cybersecurity is a critical issue that affects everyone, and we want to help Pateros City residents stay safe online.



Our campaign has three main goals

Education

We want to provide Pateros City residents with the knowledge and skills they need to protect themselves online. We will offer a series of workshops, webinars, and other educational resources that cover topics such as password management, phishing prevention, and safe browsing practices.

Awareness

We want to raise awareness about the importance of cybersecurity in Pateros City. We will work with local organizations, schools, and businesses to promote cybersecurity best practices and to encourage residents to take steps to protect themselves online.

Community

We want to build a strong and supportive cybersecurity community in Pateros City. We will create opportunities for residents to connect, share ideas, and learn from each other. We will also work to establish partnerships with local organizations and businesses to ensure that our campaign has a lasting impact.

Our mission

   Our mission is to educate our community about common cybersecurity risks such as phishing, malware, and other online threats. We aim to promote safe online practices that will help safeguard our personal information and community’s digital infrastructure.

Our Vision

   At SecuPateros, we envision a world where the younger generation is equipped with the knowledge and skills to navigate the digital world safely. Our goal is to empower students with the tools they need to protect themselves and their communities from cyber threats.
